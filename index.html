About

CEREBRA is a stylized indie boss-survival experience focused on adaptive enemy behavior, prediction pressure, and post-run analysis.

Privacy

This build stores best score, accessibility settings, and AI memory locally in your browser for gameplay continuity.

Step 1 Move, dash, read warnings

Use WASD or Arrow Keys to move. Dash with Space or Shift to break pressure waves, lane strikes, and predictive traps.

Step 2 Break the AI's assumptions

CEREBRA studies your repeated direction changes and classifies your playstyle. Change tempo to stop giving it easy reads.

Step 3 Heal, survive, then breach

Blue shards heal you. In Ascension, golden breach shards charge the core objective. Hold the central upload ring to win.

What Are Echoes?

Echoes are corruption zones created from previous deaths. They are the AI's memory made physical. Resetting memory clears them.
